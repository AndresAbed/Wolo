See some of the awesome activities you can do

See The Grand Canyon

See the Grand Canyon

Considered one of the greatest tourist attractions in the United States, this is not your average canyon. Boasting an elevation change of 7,000 feet and some of the best hiking and whitewater rafting on this side of the Rockies should earn this beauty a high spot on your list.

Experience Coachella

Experience Coachella

Coachella will always hold a special place in the hearts of Wolo founders Mike and Ray. And why wouldnt it? You get to spend an entire weekend listening to the best musicians in the world with 100,000 amazing people. Did we mention that it's in the beautiful Coachella valley against a breathtaking backdrop of Palm trees and mountains?

Experience Glastonbury in England

Experience Glastonbury in England

Regarded as one of the best music festivals in the world, Glastonbury has been blowing people's minds since 1970. This five day festival sees 175,000 music enthusiasts every June. Glastonbury must be on your bucket list if you are a music festival lover!

Learn Spanish

Learn Spanish

Más guacamole, por favor! If you love Mexican food, but have no idea what that means, you’ve come to the right place. Join the Wolo community and find friends to practice your Español. You’ll be ordering your next burrito like a pro in no time.

Visit Machu Picchu

Visit Machu Picchu

Recently declared the world’s top landmark, this wonder of the modern world is perched high in the Andes Mountains of Peru. A breathtaking experience that will literally leave you short of breath if you’re not careful, altitude anyone?

Go to Oktoberfest

Go to Oktoberfest

Do you enjoy delicious German beer, Würstl and Blaukraut? Do you want to do your part in helping drink the 8 million liters of beer consumed each year during the world’s largest beer festival? We thought so. We also think it’s about time you stopped dilly dallying and started planning your epic trip. Prost!

Backpack Patagonia

Backpack Patagonia

Patagonia is the ultimate fantasy for anyone interested in hiking. Situated in the south of Chile and Argentina, Patagonia consists of craggy mountains crowned with glaciers and glistening with waterfalls. The glacial lakes, flowering meadows, marshlands, and windblown cliffs that skirt the Magellan Strait present countless opportunities for exploration on foot. The best way to see the breathtaking landscapes is of course with equally excited and fun people! Form your trekking party with Wolo and get going!

Go Skydiving

Go Skydiving

You’re not afraid of heights, are you? Don’t worry, so is just about everyone else on this planet. Lucky for you, there is now an easy way to meet awesome people to conquer your fears with!
